# Mission: Swedish - 12-Week Intensive
**Agent:** Dan
**Destination:** Gothenburg, Sweden (August Arrival)
**Daily Training Target:** 20 Minutes

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
*Focus: Phonetics, basic greetings, the "Fika" culture, and "The Big Two" (En/Ett).*

- **Week 1:** Pronunciation & Basics (Hej, Hejdå, Tack, Förlåt).
- **Week 2:** The "To Be" and "To Have" verbs (Är, Har) + Personal Pronouns.
- **Week 3:** Food & Drink (Essential for Gothenburg's café scene).
- **Week 4:** Numbers 0-100 and Time.

## Phase 2: Survival (Weeks 5-8)
*Focus: Navigation, Shopping, and Housing.*

- **Week 5:** Directions & Trams (Västtrafik survival).
- **Week 6:** Simple Present Tense Verbs.
- **Week 7:** Questions (Vem, Vad, När, Var, Varför).
- **Week 8:** Colors, Adjectives, and basic descriptions.

## Phase 3: Integration (Weeks 9-12)
*Focus: Family, Work, and Local Gothenburg slang.*

- **Week 9:** Family members & Introductions.
- **Week 10:** Common Gothenburg phrases (e.g., "Gôtt mos").
- **Week 11:** Future Tense (Planning your move).
- **Week 12:** Review & Final Briefing.
